About Us

Since 2021, we have been working with dogs of all types, sizes, and ages. With the addition of our dalmatian, Colby, in 2022, we have been taking amazing care of pups all over Middlesex County.

Our passion for taking care of dogs began in 2003 when we adopted our first dog: Samson, a very energetic and smart dalmatian mix. As you can tell, we have fallen in love with the breed ever since.

In 2021 we decided to create Suburban Pooch and have been bulding a great community since. We love our clients like they are a part of the family (that includes our human clients too!). We are a family owned small business and community members.

We are fully insured and bonded, as well as CPR Certified. So rest assured your pooch is in great hands!
